Basil Pesto

Yields approximately 9 oz

Add this to your favorite pasta, over bread or on top of your favorite pizza!

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons grated pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Crush garlic using a mortar and pestle (you can also use a food processor or blender). Add pine nuts; crush with the garlic. Add basil leaves gradually, making circular movements with the pestle, until a smooth paste forms.
  2. Mix Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese and pecorino Romano cheese using a wooden spoon. Stir in olive oil until pesto is blended.
